%-------------------------------------------------------------------------%
This archive contains the  Primal-Dual (PD) solvers applied to the dynamical optimal transport problem
discretized on two staggered grids using the Helmholtz-Hodge (HH) decomposition.

It can be tested with:

PDHH
%-------------------------------------------------------------------------%
What to change to test the code:

1) Choice of the densities we want to interpolate: rho0 and rho1

2) Choice of the dimension of the problem: NxP (spatial discretization) and Q (time discretization)

3) Choice of the parameters of the primal-dual algorithm: sigma, itmax,
